Background

Kobe Bryant
Gianna Bryant
Kobe Bryant was born on August 23, 1978, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, to Joe and Pamela Bryant and grew up with two older sisters, Sharia and Shaya. He spent part of his childhood in Italy while his father played professional basketball there. Kobe married Vanessa Laine in 2001, and together they had four daughters: Natalia, Gianna, Bianka, and Capri. Over his 20-year career with the Los Angeles Lakers, he won five NBA championships, earned two Finals MVPs, and was an 18-time All-Star. Tragically, Kobe and his daughter Gianna died in a helicopter crash on January 26, 2020, leaving behind a loving family and a lasting legacy.
